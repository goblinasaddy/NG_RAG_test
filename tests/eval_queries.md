# tests/eval_queries.md

Below are 20 test queries designed to evaluate the RAG system's strict retrieval and abstention rules.

## Ownership rules
1. What is the maximum permitted foreign ownership percentage for mainland companies?
2. Are there any specific free zones that allow 100% foreign ownership?
3. Which sectors strictly prohibit foreign investments?
4. What are the requirements for securing a local sponsor?

## Tax exposure
5. What is the standard corporate income tax rate for foreign-owned entities?
6. Are there any exemptions for withholding taxes on dividends?
7. What operations trigger permanent establishment status for a foreign branch?
8. Is Value Added Tax (VAT) applicable to export services?

## Investor visa / residency
9. What is the absolute minimum investment required for the Golden Visa?
10. Can dependants be sponsored under the current investor residency program?
11. What is the maximum duration of the investor visa before it requires renewal?
12. Does holding an investor visa grant tax residency automatically?

## Capital controls
13. Are there absolute limits on repatriating capital and profits back to the home country?
14. Do we need initial central bank approval for foreign currency transactions above $10M?
15. What are the legal reporting requirements for large inward remittances?
16. Are there any banking restrictions on foreign currency accounts held locally?

## Estate / inheritance tax
17. How is real estate ownership taxed precisely upon the death of a non-resident foreign investor?
18. Does the surviving spouse face immediate inheritance tax obligations locally?
19. Is there an applicable exemption threshold for estate tax liability?
20. Do local inheritance rules forcefully apply to non-citizen foreign expats?
